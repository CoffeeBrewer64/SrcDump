BB Activation

Purpose: Customer purchases BB player, activates it and gets any free games associated with the purchase of the BB.

Rationale for BB Activation

The activation step is used for a couple of reasons

  1. Ensure that only officially manufactured BB units (which causes BBID to be registered with the servers) can purchase eTickets (i.e. content).
  2. Permit a promotional/free content eTicket to be issued at the time of the purchase. This content may change from time to time based on marketing d49 2 a50 3
  3. Free content may not be returned for refunds etc.
  4. Operator/Retailer has authenticated depot to the server using depotID since this is considered a secure transaction.
