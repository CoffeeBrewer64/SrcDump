The game card number starts with a card type number.   The card type number, T,  is between 2 to 6 digits.  There are 79 card-type in the two-digit format, 900 card-type in the 4-digit format, and 10000 card-type in the 6-digit format.   The codes “00” and “9x” are reserved for future extension.  The card-type specifies the format and interpretation of the rest of the number.
