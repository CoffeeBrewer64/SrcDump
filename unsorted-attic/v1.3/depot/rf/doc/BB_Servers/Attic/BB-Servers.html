BB Servers

The following diagram shows all of the servers and the associated data stores. The list of transactions initiated by these servers is given in the BB-Transactions document.

Servers

Content Management Servers (CMS)
  • The CMS registers studios/developers and interfaces with the Release Kits given to studios.
  • When content is released by the studios,  it is uploaded to the CMS where it can undergo validation and transformation before it is published to the XS & CDS for further sale & distribution.
Transactional Servers (XS)
  1. The Transactional Subsystem is central to the server complex. It decides when to make content available for purchase and is responsible for BB Player sales and content/eTicket sales.
  2. Records activities with BB devices for auditing purpose.
  3. Does not distribute content itself.
  4. Gift Certificates will not be implemented in the first round.
Content Distribution Servers (CDS)
  1. Receives content from the Content Management Servers
  2. Content synchronization & downloads available to the Depots
  3. Never initiates contact with the Depot, relies on RMS to do that if necessary.
  4. Authenticates Depot based on hardwareID only.
Manufacturing Servers
  1. Co-located at the chip manufacturer and the BB Player manufacturer.
  2. Communicates to the CA Servers the  list of BBIDs and public keys.
Certificate Authority Servers
  1. Manages eTicket licenses and maintains revocation lists. 
Remote Management Servers (RMS)
  1. User interface to manage BB Depot remotely from single point
  2. Always has the latest IP address for each Depot and is able to contact the Depot via RFRMP or equivalent mechanism.
  3. Maintains <hardwareID, region-ID, IP Address> mappings.
  4. Depots get to know about RMS subsystem during depot installation procedures.
Billing & Account Management Servers
  1. Repository and a set of software to manage BB-Depot Accounts, which are effectively retailers.
  2. Items to manage are; BB and hardware option sales, inventory of BB and hardware options, eTicket issuance records, and content download records.
Audit Servers
  1. Perform auditing in batch
  2. Use audit records from all operational systems to check any violations to license or usage of BB Depot or BB Players.
  3. Implement some heuristics to detect violations
  4. Generates report of auditing performed
Release Kit Servers
  1. A tool to given to Studios to develop or localize title content
