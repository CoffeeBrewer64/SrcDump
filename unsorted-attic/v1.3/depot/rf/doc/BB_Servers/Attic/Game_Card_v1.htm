What’s E-money?

E-money card (similar to Phone card) is a piece of paper or plastic with a concealed random number, called the E-money Code.  The E-money cards are physical goods sold by the retailer, and distributed using the BB Player's distribution channel.   The E-money card is single-use, i.e.,  it has no value after the first use and it cannot be recharged.    Currently E-money is limited to game purchase, although future extension is possible,  it is beyond the scope of this document.
