    Content Download

    Description: Customer downloads content (redeems eTicket) into his player's BB Card.

    Rationale

    1. Customer may own a large number of eTickets, but have space for a limited number of content on his BB Card. So, he may constantly be deleting and reloading content into the BB Card from his virtual Safety Box.
    2. Content download is a separate action from eTicket Purchase, since this may take a slightly longer period of time.
    3. By downloading the content of a game, the customer agrees that the corresponding eTicket is no longer refundable.

    Assumptions & conditions

    1. The BB Card identifies (does not authenticate) the BB Player for which the eTicket has been. The actual BB Player does not need to be present.
    2. The BB Player owns at least one eTicket which identifies the content.
    3. In order to complete download of the initial Free Content associated with the purchase of a BB Player, the customer has to open the packaging and retrieve the BB Card.
    4. Retailer may have to authenticate the depot the server unless the customer has downloaded that content before.

    Sequence of events/actions

    Customer Retailer
    1. Inserts his BB Card into the Download Station
    2. Reviews all owned eTickets
    3. Using the BB controller on the Download Station, customer selects content to be downloaded.
    4. Waits for the display to indicate completion of the download process
    5. Repeats this process for all content that he is interested in that he owns
    6. Removes BB Card.
