        Assumptions & conditions

        1. A refund will not be permitted if the eTicket is downloaded (eTicket has been redeemed) from the Server into the BB Card.
        2. The customer also has only 30 minutes after the purchase of an eTicket to get a refund. This is to limit the exposure we have where another customer#2 with a BB Card that contains the BB identification of customer#1's BB Player asks for a refund for a recently purchased eTicket by customer#1.  The time limit must be a BB System configurable option.
        3. The Server always has the complete information about the BB Player & eTickets that are owned by it.
        4. The BB Card identifies (does not authenticate) the BB Player for which the eTicket has been purchased.
        5. Retailer has authenticated depot to the Server using DepotID.
        6. A refund is only permitted from the same Store (as identified by the DepotID) where the eTicket Purchase was made.
